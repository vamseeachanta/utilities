Services

Subsea systems is a custom equipment manufacturer serving the energy sectors consisting of refining, petrochemical, offshore, subsea and related industries.

Turnkey services

We offer turnkey manufacturing solutions outlined in our custom manufacturing process flowchart below. Third party inspections and certifications will be accommodated and coordinated all throughout the process as required meet customer certification requirements.

We perform welding, PWHT, fabrication and inspection of metal products (new and re-manufactured). We partner with suitable and qualified vendors to complement our core capabilities and thus providing turnkey solutions.

Manufacturing and Fabrication

Subsea Systems has many Welding procedures (for GTAW, GMAW, FCAW, SMAW and SAW) qualified to various codes including: ASME Section IX, API 6A, API 1104, NACE MR0175/ISO 15156:2009, and AWS D1.1, etc. Subsea Systems’ engineer and project management team works with the customer to streamline the fabrication process and have their project out on time.

Offshore equipment

We manufacture, test, repair and refurbish offshore equipment including:

  • Tension ring
  • Telescopic joints
  • Riser spider refurbishment
  • Full length drilling riser joints
  • Riser cross-over joints
  • Drilling riser joints refurbishment and maintenance
  • Offshore skids
  • Offshore shipping containers

Onshore equipment

We manufacture, test, repair and refurbish onshore equipment including:

  • Valves
  • Close tolerance components
  • Pressure valves

Heavy projects

  • Handle and work with heavy components to manufacture, repair and/or refurbishment
  • Ability to mobilize required accessories such as heavy equipment cranes etc. through qualified vendors
  • Ability to test by partnering with qualified vendors

Skid Mounts and Support Structures

  • Skid mount units to help in transport and storage of equipment
  • Designed with attatchment points such as hooks, chains, rails etc. for handling and sliding in place

Offshore shipping containers

  • Offshore shipping containers for transport amd storage of equipment.
  • Designed to DNV2.7-1 (or European standard EN12079)
  • Custom designed to meet additional strength and design requirements
  • Custom designed with attachment points such as pad eyes etc. for offshore handing and transportation

Pressure Vessels and Piping

  • Ability to design and manufacture with ASME Section VIII Div. 1 Certification and meet customer specifications.

Rolling and Forming

  • Perform rolling and forming metalwork’s through partnership with qualified vendors
  • Good understanding of temperature effect on physical properties for all metalwork’s.

Pressure Test

  • Pressure test equipment up to 25,000 psi
  • Ability to pressure test full length drilling riser joints up to 90 ft long

Pull Test

Ability to perform pull tests by coordinating with the qualified vendors.
